Frequently Asked Questions

What is PickMyBinge.com?

PickMyBinge.com is a smart recommendation tool designed to help you quickly discover binge-worthy movies and TV shows. We use filters for genre, popularity, and ratings to help you stop scrolling and start watching.

Is this website free to use?

Yes, PickMyBinge.com is 100% free for everyone. The site is supported by on-page advertisements and affiliate links, which means we may earn a small commission if you sign up for a service we recommend, at no extra cost to you.

How do you get your movie and TV show data?

Our data, including titles, posters, ratings, and release dates, is generously provided by The Movie Database (TMDB). It is a massive, community-built database that ensures our recommendations are always up-to-date.
